About Me

Thanks for visiting my website! My name is Aidan, and I plan on majoring in Marketing at BYU. I'm passionate about my religion, family, marketing, entrepreneurship, and building meaningful connections with others.

I’ve always been fascinated by what appeals to the human mind and how to effectively reach people. Throughout High School, I grew various social media accounts to over 500,000 followers, and helped market friends' and close one’s businesses. I fell in love with the analytics side of social media—studying the patterns, testing different ideas, and learning to navigate the unique algorithms of each platform.

I'm interested in pursuing a career in marketing or entrepreneurship. My ultimate goal is to be able to comfortably provide for my future family while doing something I love.
