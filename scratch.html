How I Flip a Card

  1. Research the Market
    • Check recent sold listings across various platforms
    • Take into account player production where others may not
    • Estimate value of card based on similar ones in the case of vague info or lack of sales
  2. Buy the Right Cards
    • Buy easy to move or "liquid" cards
    • Buy at the bottom of a players market
    • Don't fall victim to hype or "FOMO"
    • Identify historic trends and use them to my advantage (ie. players markets jumping after being drafted or before playoffs)
  3. Resell at the Right Time
    • Use hype and FOMO to my advantage, letting others buy into it
    • Resell at the beggining of the sports season, maximizing profits while removing risk
    • Don't gamble on a player. If there's a profit to be made, take it
